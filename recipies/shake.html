Description

Tasty and high-protein peanut butter shake

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Peanut Butter
  • Frozen Fruit of your choice
  • Banana
  • Protein powder

Steps to make:

  1. Add milk, 3 tbs of peanut butter, frozen fruit, banana, and one serving of protein powder into a blender
  2. Blend and enjoy
